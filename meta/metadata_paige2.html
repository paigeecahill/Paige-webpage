All about my trip to Hawaii

Why everyone should go to Hawaii!

I went to Hawaii when I was in 7th grade. I went for 2 and a half weeks. I stayed at a house with my family and we had a blast. The weather when we went was so beautiful. It only rained once or twice and only for about 5 minutes.

The beaches in Hawaii were so nice. One day we went snorkeling and saw many fish and turtles. Another day we were zip linning which was also very fun.

We spent most of the days in Hawaii relaxing on the beach. When you would go in the water, just to swim, you would see many fish, turtles, and even some seals! Most of the beaches had snow cone places that you would able to walk to which was nice when you wanted something refreshing

Overall Hawaii was a very fun and enjoyable trip! I recommend everyone to go to Hawaii on vacation at some point in their life!

Here are some activities you can do in Hawaii:

  • Surfing
  • Zip-lining
  • Snorkeling
